hi hello greetings, I'm helen!

I work on machine learning somewhere new. Previously, I was a machine learning engineer at Dessa, a research fellow studying technology & smart cities for Sidewalk Labs & Waterfront Toronto, and a data scientist in the telecommunications industry.

I organize the Toronto Women's Data Group. Previously, I was part of the steering committee for the Toronto Deep Learning Series, and an editorial associate for Towards Data Science.

My current interests are in machine learning for language. I also spent time as a Fellow at the Recurse Center in New York City studying misinformation and our information ecosystem.

I enjoy poetry, writing, coffee, artificial intelligence policy, and building unnecessary things with machine learning.
